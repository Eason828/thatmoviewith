That Movie With

Find common movies among actors.

1.

Add Actors

Tap anywhere on the screen to add actors. Swipe right on an actor to remove them.

2.

See Common Movies

After you've added actors, swipe left to view the movies they've starred in together.

3.

View IMDb Info

Tap on a movie to open its IMDb page. You can even open the movie in the IMDb app by selecting "Open in IMDb" in the share menu.
